PrayerTimesSG

Daily prayer times for Singapore 🇸🇬

About

PrayerTimesSG provides accurate, reliable, and easy-to-access prayer times for the Muslim community in Singapore.

This project started from a simple need. A friend who does delivery work wanted a quick, distraction-free way to glance at daily prayer times while on the move. Over time, it quietly grew into a tool used by others — shaped by clarity, trust, and simplicity.

Prayer times should be something you glance at and move on with your day, not something you struggle to find, buried under clutter or unnecessary features.

Philosophy

PrayerTimesSG is built on three simple ideas:

1. Simple

The app does one thing, which is to show prayer times, and tries to do it well. The architecture is kept lightweight, the interface is clean, and the experience stays fast.

2. Clutter-free

No ads. No pop-ups. No features added "just because." Everything you see has a reason to exist.

3. Trusted

Prayer times are based on official MUIS data. The system makes its status and data freshness visible, so reliability is clear at a glance.

PrayerTimesSG is designed to be there when you need it, and invisible when you don't.

Features

  • Daily Updates
  • MUIS Official Data
  • Mobile Optimised
  • Auto Refresh at Midnight
  • Singapore Timezone
  • System Health Monitor
